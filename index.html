Who am I?

Hello. My name is Mikhail Vasilyev. I was born and live in Volgograd (perhaps you may know its as Stalingrad) with my family. For more than ten years I have been working in Internet service provider companies. Now I am a networking engineer in the POWERNET, where I control a little piece of the Internet, also known as Autonomous System 51032.

I am Cisco certified CCNP & CCDP. Sometimes I write for Habr blog, and also you can find me on my public Telegram channel. Besides that, I give a part of my time to the "Map of Volgograd ISPs" project. And I spend the rest of the time with my family, which I love so much.

You can easily contact me by any of the ways listed on the first page. Thank you for paying attention and reading about me. :)
